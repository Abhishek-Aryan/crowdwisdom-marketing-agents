# CrowdWisdomTrading — Agent Context

## Product
CrowdWisdomTrading.com is an AI-powered market intelligence newsletter.
It aggregates predictions and analysis from 5,600+ professional traders 
across YouTube, Reddit, X, and trading communities (50M+ combined followers).
Subscribers receive weekly briefings with high-conviction trade ideas, 
crowd consensus signals, and market briefings — saving them 100+ hours/week.

## Plans
- Weekly plan: basic briefing
- Pro plan: daily signals, entry/stop/target levels

## Unique Value Proposition
Unlike single analysts or black-box algos, CrowdWisdom is the distilled 
consensus of thousands of real traders — fully transparent track record.

## Target Customer
Retail traders who place their own trades, feel overwhelmed by information,
want edge without spending hours on research.

## Core Pain Points of Target Audience
- Information overload (100s of trading YouTubers, Twitter accounts)
- Fear of missing signals
- Lack of time to research
- Trading alone with no consensus view
- Burned by following single-analyst hot takes

## Competitors
- Motley Fool (expensive, stock-picker focused)
- Seeking Alpha (text heavy, subscription)
- Trading Discord groups (noisy, unverified)
- Individual trading YouTubers (one perspective only)
